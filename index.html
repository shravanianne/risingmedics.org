About Us

The Rising Medics Initiative is run by two girls with a dream of providing underprivileged areas with free access to healthcare and medical supplies. We aim to partner with different organizations to raise money for redistribution. We also hope to educate high school students about biomedical engineering and medicine, as well as spread awareness about current issues in our world today. Join us in helping to make our dream come true, for every life we can touch.
